Configuring TinyMCE:Simple Setup

Jun 26th, 2008

Everybody is already familiar with WYSIWYG editor. Before, doing CMS projects I used to use fckeditor, and I always had a problem with its bad design and loading time. Though you can config the editor to just the actions you need. I still felt something was not right about it. And I searched about editors, then I came across TinyMCE editor. TinyMCE is platform independent, javascript based editor and has a very special feature of changing the textareas into the editor’s instance. It takes lesser time then fckeditor or other editors I have used. It also has a simple look and can fit into any designs. And its pretty easy to configure too.
